About

CIE South Korea

The Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (CIE) South Korea is the South Korean chapter of CIE Global, established to coordinate and scale innovation, entrepreneurship, and global market-access initiatives originating from South Korea.

CIE South Korea focuses on leveraging South Korea’s advanced innovation capacity, industrial ecosystems, and skilled talent base to support enterprise expansion, technology collaboration, and strategic partnerships across global markets, including emerging and growth economies.

Operating within CIE’s global coordination framework, CIE South Korea serves as a national coordination and partnership platform, aligning Korean innovators, enterprises, institutions, and partners with structured international opportunities and impact-driven initiatives.

Rollout

A phased, adaptive implementation approach

Phased Rollout & Adaptive Implementation

CIE South Korea adopts a phased and adaptive implementation approach, aligned with CIE Global’s coordination framework. The approach begins with ecosystem mapping, stakeholder engagement, and partnership alignment, followed by the progressive activation of programmes and initiatives tailored to identified global opportunities.

Implementation is coordinated in collaboration with Korean institutions, enterprises, and international partners, with digital platforms and programme tools deployed incrementally as initiatives mature. This approach ensures flexibility, coherence, and long-term sustainability while enabling measurable global impact.
